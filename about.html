О нас

Upgraded Points (“UP”) was launched in 2016 by founder Alex Miller, with the mission to not only show travelers the real value of points and miles, but to also upgrade their overall travel experiences. UP works to achieve this goal by publishing free, highly researched, in-depth and intuitive content that aims to give our readers everything they’re looking for — and more.

Our experts often spend hours and hours researching and crafting a single piece of content. The team doesn’t just publish content, though. With the airline and hotel industry constantly evolving and changing, content quickly becomes outdated. That’s why we review and update every single article on the site, regularly – some as often as every month.

We love hearing from readers who were able to take fantastic adventures or experience great products they wouldn’t have otherwise managed, had it not been for the content we produce and share. That’s why UP exists and that’s what excites us. The brand has grown very quickly since launching in 2016, welcoming ~20MM visitors in 2021 with increasing press and brand mentions.
